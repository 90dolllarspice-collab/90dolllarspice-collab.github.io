Before/after photos, notes on repairs, and optional labeling so install is straightforward.

FAQ

Answers to the questions that cause delays.

What do you need from me to quote accurately?

Photos of the damage area(s), vehicle/year/engine or platform, what symptoms you’re seeing, and whether any prior repairs were attempted.

Do you do full custom harness builds?

This page is set up for restoration/repair/rework. If you do full builds, add it as a service and specify the platforms you support.

How long does it take?

Turnaround depends on damage severity and parts availability. After intake, you’ll get a written estimate with a timeline.

Can you diagnose my problem without the car?

We can verify harness integrity on the bench (continuity/pin-out checks). Vehicle-side faults still require on-vehicle diagnostics.

Do you accept ship-ins?

Yes. Pack the harness to prevent crushing, include your contact details, and a short description of the issue and vehicle info.
