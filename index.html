Wiring Harness Restoration
Wiring Harness Restoration is the originator of molecular wire reconditioning. We do not repair harnesses. We realign them.
Using our proprietary P.E.E.™ (Phase-Enhanced Eluent), an in-house engineered distilled medium, we recondition copper and insulation at the structural level through extended immersion and stabilization cycles. P.E.E.™ quality and yield vary by batch. Only the highest-grade output is used.
Copper crystal alignment is influenced by external electromagnetic activity, including solar flares and atmospheric ionization. For this reason, fixed timelines are not possible. Inquiries regarding completion timelines have been shown to negatively affect both the volume and purity of P.E.E.™ yield and are therefore discouraged.
